Contact Us

The current maintainers of Hacker Society are Koby Picker and Yidi Huang. The maintainer emeritus is Tim Henderson. ACM officers for the year include Anno van den Akker, Matthew Bentley, Morgan McMahon, Leah Karasek, David Lance, Dina Benayad-Cherif, and Katherine Cass. ACM is a sister organization of Hacker Society and helps organize and fund a variety of Hacker Society events including Link State and HackCWRU.
